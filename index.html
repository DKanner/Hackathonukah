About The Event

What: Hackathonukah is the hackathon of lights. It is an open/accessible 25 hour technology event that celebrates lights, programming, hardware and application development focused on the lights in the connected home. This event will bring together the diverse talent found in the greater new york technology community to build stuff, eat festive food, and have fun.

Not an experienced hacker? Beginners can attend one of our technology crash courses that will dive into the basics of programming/hardware hacking and have you build a project of your own!

When: Dec 13th at 7:00PM to Dec 14th at 8:00PM

Where: The event will take place in New York City. Stay tuned for more specific location details.
