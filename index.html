About The Event

Hackathonukah is the hackathon of lights. It is a 25 hour technology event that celebrates lights, programming, hardware, and technology. This event will bring together the diverse talent found in the greater New York technology community to build stuff, eat festive food, and have fun.

Not an experienced hacker? Beginners can attend one of our technology crash courses that will dive into the basics of programming/hardware hacking and have you build a project of your own!

When: 2014-12-13 / 7:00PM to 2014-12-14 / 8:00PM

Where: New York City. Stay tuned for more specific location details.
